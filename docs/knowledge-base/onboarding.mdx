# üöÄ Onboarding
</FloatInPlace>

<FloatInLeft>
In any professional system, onboarding represents the **first meaningful interaction** between contributor and framework. At Cumulus, this experience is not reduced to a passive greeting or list of links. Instead, onboarding acts as a **contextual launch platform**, tailored to your **role**, **confidence level**, and **real-world goals**. The intent is not to show everything, but to show what matters‚Äîat the right time, for the right person.
</FloatInLeft>

<FloatInRight>
From the very first interaction, the system prioritizes **clarity**, **structured progression**, and **user autonomy**. Designers should not waste time guessing where spacing tokens are located. Engineers should not need to reverse-engineer prop behavior. Product stakeholders should never be disconnected from interface rationale. Onboarding at Cumulus is built to **accelerate contribution**, not delay it.
</FloatInRight>
